# Full-Scale ERP Solution Blueprint (COMPLETED)

## Overview
A comprehensive, professional-grade ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) and BPA (Business Process Automation) solution tailored for Manufacturing and Corporate Enterprises. The application integrates critical business operations, focusing on efficiency, automation, and data-driven insights.

## Core Modules (IMPLEMENTED)
- **Manufacturing:** Production floor monitoring, Bill of Materials (BOM), and resource scheduling.
- **BPA & Workflow:** Intelligent automation of business processes (Lead-to-Cash, Procure-to-Pay).
- **Sales & Billing:** GST-compliant invoicing, revenue tracking in INR (₹), and sales analytics.
- **Accounting:** Comprehensive financial management, expense tracking, and Indian tax (GST) reporting.
- **Inventory:** Real-time stock management with supplier integration across PAN-India warehouses.
- **Customer Relations:** Multi-tier enterprise client management with GSTIN profiling.
- **Payroll:** Integrated payroll management with statutory compliance (PF, ESIC).
